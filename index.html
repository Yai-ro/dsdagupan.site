Who We Are

Our Vision

We envision Dominican School, Dagupan as an evolving center of character formation and academic excellence that nurtures Learners into becoming transformative leaders of global society, who are bearers of TRUTH in the service of God, country and fellowmen.

Our Mission

As a Catholic, Filipino and Dominican School, we commit ourselves to nurture our Christ-like attitude, deepen our devotion to the Blessed Mother, protect God's creation, participate in social transformation, bring the TRUTH wherever we go and live the Dominican Spirit of STUDY, for the benefit of the society.

WE, DOMINICANS bear witness to the TRUTH in the SERVICE of GOD and His CREATION.

Core Values

The Dominican educational tradition is rooted in the pursuit of Truth (Veritas) through study, prayer, community, and preaching. These four pillars guide every aspect of school life — from the classroom to the community.

"Veritas Et Excellentia"
